Rental Qualifications
Effective Effective April 9, 2014

  • - One Week gross salary must be equivalent to, or greater than the monthly rent.
  • - A completed application is required.
  • - $100 Application & Admin fee must be paid *PER PERSON*
  • -$250 – $950 Security Deposit must be paid.
  • - 2 of your most current pay stubs
  • - A black & white copy of your driver’s license or photo ID
  • - All applicants must be at least 21 years of age*
  • - No undergraduate students accepted unless over 21.
  • - Occupancy restrictions are as follows:
    - 1 Bedroom 1-2 persons
    - 2 Bedroom No more than 4 people
  • - A 525 or higher credit score & favorable background check.

The Security Deposit will be forfeited after 24 hours if you should decide not to fulfill your rental agreement
