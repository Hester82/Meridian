From Raleigh/Wilson:

Stay on Hwy 264 as it turns into Stantonsburg Road, this will lead you to Arlington Blvd, where you will make a right. Meridian Park will be located shortly on the right.

From New Bern:

Stay on Hwy 43 as it turns into Charles Blvd. Make a left on Red Banks Road, then make a right on Arlington Blvd. Follow Arlington until you come to Meridian Park on the left.

From Washington:

Turn left onto Hwy 264 Alternate/Greenville Blvd as you come into town. Stay on Greenville Blvd until you reach Arlington Blvd, where you will make a right. Follow Arlington until you reach Meridian Park on the left.
